Contentious Districts

According to data from 270ToWin, the following districts were labelled as 'contentious' in 2022.

In investigating these districts a bit further, we can see a relatively normal distribution of urbanicity, with the majority of these districts classifying as 'Suburban-Exurban'. Interestingly, in looking at how their partisan lean has changed, both on an individual level through the tooltip visualizations, as well as by the combined chart, it becomes clear that many of these districts underwent a fairly significant partisan lean change in the past year.

In these districts, the incumbent party was not considered 'safe' and house elections were close. The filter option on the map allows exploration of which states remained with their incumbent party for the 2022 midterm elections and which did not.
